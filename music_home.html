Alex Marrero is an Austin, TX based musician, songwriter, producer, teacher, and bi-lingual voice over talent. Born in Mexico City to Cuban parents, Alex got his professional start playing clubs in Mexico at the age of 15 and has continued his musical evolution since moving to Austin in 1994.

Alex is a respected vocalist, multi-instrumentalist and sought after sideman in various genres. Alex is known for comfortably inhabiting the role of drummer, percussionist, guitar player, background singer, writer or producer. As a lead singer he possesses a unique bravado which has garnered him a reputation as an engaging and charismatic front man. His voice and showmanship present in “Brownout Presents: Brown Sabbath” earned him critical & public acclaim as well as high praise from Ozzy Osbourne himself.

Alex has performed and collaborated with: Grupo Fantasma, Brownout, Bob Schneider, Bo Deans, Los Lobos, Coyle Girelli, Carrie Rodriguez, Charanga Cakewalk, Gary Clark Jr, Darden Smith, Gina Chavez, Greyhounds, Papa Mali, Kalu James, Topaz & many more.
