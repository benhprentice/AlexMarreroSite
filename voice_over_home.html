Alex Marrero

La Voz

Audio Samples: http://www.voice123.com/alexmarrero

Bilingual Voice Over Artist and Singer from Mexico City, based in Austin, TX. Over 25 years experience in the industry. Alex works out of his professional home studio equipped with Source Connect. He provides narration for commercials, audio books, podcasts, apps, educational videos and beyond!

Alex can use a rich baritone for classic narration/announcer work. But he is very versatile, Alex effortlessly delivers conversational, friendly, unassuming, natural, guy next door, intense, unique and wry performances. As a vocalist you get passionate & memorable performances, Alex has a very broad vocal range.

Clients include: Humana, Duolingo, Maestro Dobel Tequila, Sabritas/Lays, National Public Radio, Corona Beer, The State of New York, The State of Kentucky, The State of Texas, The State of Florida, Texas Dept. of Transportation, Summit K12, AT&T, Southwest Airlines, RC Cola, Valero, & many more.

Alex is the bilingual voice of CapMetro Buses & Trains in the City of Austin, Texas.

Alex also does copy translation/adaptation services.
